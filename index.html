ABOUT ME

I'm a Research Engineer / Software Developer with a wide range of interests and experience which, in part, I hope to convey through this website. My real goal is to use this site as a working Research Notebook; a place to gather my thoughts by writing down notes, summaries and opinions of anything interesting that I come across.

In my career I've learned, from those more experienced than I, that a researcher depends on a notebook that is filled with just about anything that he comes across. Research is a creative process and like any creative process one needs experimentation, analysis and documentation to build upon. There also needs to be a place for musings and references to other bodies of knowledge.
